#####Body#####

1. Northern Nanny,
Or: the loving Lasses lamentation,In this same Sonnet she doth plain discoverThe true affection of a saithful Lover,Wailing his abscence since by fortune cruelShe was deprived of her dearest Jewel,Which may a pattern to all lovers beTo take example by this maiden free. Tune of, In January last, &c.
